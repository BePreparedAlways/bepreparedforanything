Why Is It Important to Be Prepared?

Emergencies can happen at any time. Storms, fires, floods, and power outages are things we cannot always control. But we can control how prepared we are! Being prepared helps keep us and our families safe. When we plan ahead, we feel calmer and more confident.

Types of Emergencies

  • Severe storms and hurricanes
  • Floods
  • Fires
  • Earthquakes
  • Power outages

Knowing what kinds of emergencies can happen helps us get ready for them.

What Should Be in an Emergency Kit?

  • Water (at least 1 gallon per person per day)
  • Non-perishable food (like canned food)
  • Flashlight
  • Extra batteries
  • First aid kit
  • Blanket
  • Important phone numbers

Try building a small emergency kit at home with your family!

Make a Family Emergency Plan

  • Know important phone numbers
  • Choose a safe meeting place
  • Practice what to do in case of fire
  • Talk about different emergency situations

Having a plan helps everyone stay calm and safe.

Safety Quiz

2. Where should your family meet if you leave the house?

3. How much water should each person have per day?

Think about your answers and talk about them with your family!
